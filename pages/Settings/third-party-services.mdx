# Emotes

## BTTV Emotes
Enables or disables [BTTV](<https://betterttv.com/>) emotes

## Support for BTTV WEBP Emotes
Enables or disables [BTTV](<https://betterttv.com/>) emotes in the WEBP format

## FFZ Emotes
Enables or disables [FFZ](<https://www.frankerfacez.com/>) emotes

## 7TV Emotes
Enables or disables [7TV](<https://7tv.app/>) emotes

## 7TV Global Emotes
Enables or disables [7TV](<https://7tv.app/>) global emotes

## Homies emotes Emotes
Enables or disables [Homies](<https://chatterinohomies.com/>) emotes

## Emotes Quality
Does not change emote size, only quality.

# Badges

## FFZ Badges
Enables or disables FrankerFaceZ’s supporter badge

## Chaterino Badges
Enables or disables Chatterino’s supporter badge

## Homies Badges
Enables or disables Chatterino Homies supporter badge

## PurpleTV Badges
Enables or disables PurpleTV's supporter badge

## BTTV Badges
Enables or disables BetterTTV’s supporter badge

# Pronouns
Enables or disables a badge with the user's prononouns if set up. Go to https://pronouns.alejo.io/ to set your pronouns.
